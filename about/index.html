Welcome to Gopal's Garden High school

विद्यां ददाति विनयं, विनयाद् याति पात्रताम्।
पात्रत्वात् धनमाप्नोति, धनात् धर्मं ततः सुखम्॥
Translation
From knowledge comes humility, from humility comes eligibility (worthiness),
from eligibility comes real wealth with which one does good deeds leading to happiness.


Gopal’s Garden High School (GGHS) is an offering of Sri Chaitanya Educational Trust. Where its team believes that competence without good character has no real meaning; and the first step to develop a good character is to acknowledge and accept the authority of God. GGHS is a humble attempt to provide value based education to children where we blend Vedic Culture with Modern Academics to create leaders of tomorrow by giving them the right tools.
Our Mission is “Gopal’s Garden aspires to educate its students with a multitude of loving, yet disciplined learning experiences, catering to their holistic development (physical, intellectual, emotional, social & spiritual) essential for living with excellence, to create confident and responsible leaders to serve the society with benevolence”.
At Gopal's Garden High School, we are committed to be a friend, philosopher and guide in the child's 'learning journey' by providing Education that leads to Excellence which in turn paves the path for Empowerment. We give learning opportunities that enable your child to grow and develop in a holistic manner, create an environment for inquisitive, intellectual, innovative minds and instill the joy of learning through cooperation and collaboration. We aim to equip children with life skills, emotional intelligence and a sound value system that will hold them in good stead in the future. Our highly competent and experienced faculty, under the expert leadership of our Principal, Mrs. Rajas Sardesai Bhosale, inspires knowledge, learning and personal growth in our students. Our teaching-learning process is strengthened by our curriculum and learning programs, specially developed to bring out the best in every student and to empower each one to excel academically, emotionally, socially and spiritually. It is our endeavor to develop responsible young 'global citizens'.
