Curriculum


तत्कर्म यन्न बन्धाय सा विध्या या विमुक्तये|
आया साया परं कर्म विध्यडन्या शिल्पनैपुणम्
Translation
That is action, which does not not promote attachment. That is knowledge which
liberates one from bondage.
All other action is mere effort: all other knowledge is merely another skill / craftsmanship.

Since inception, the School was affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education. The School has now phased out State Board, and is affiliated to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education). The School’s first CBSE batch appeared for Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) examinations in March 2018.
At GGHS the purpose of CBSE has been more than just examine the general education of students in CBSE affiliated Schools.
The CBSE syllabus has a distinct advantage over others. The curriculum prepares the child for higher studies and competitive exams. CBSE’s flexibility allows the student to opt for subjects of his/her interest. The students have an edge over their counterparts in higher studies.
It is very essential that we move with the times and our decision to follow CBSE curriculum is one such step in this direction.
