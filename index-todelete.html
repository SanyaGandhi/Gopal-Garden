Introduction

Gopal’s Garden High School (GGHS) is an offering of Sri Chaitanya Educational Trust. Where it’s team believe that competence without good character has no real meaning; and the first step to develop a good character is to acknowledge and accept the authority of God.

Mission

Gopal’s Garden aspires to educate its students with a multitude of loving, yet disciplined learning experiences, catering to their holistic development (physical, intellectual, emotional, social & spiritual) essential for living with excellence, to create confident and responsible leaders to serve the society with benevolence.
